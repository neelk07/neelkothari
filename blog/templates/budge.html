Budge

"A Quick Way To Budget!"


What if you didn't have to enter in all that information just to keep track of your budget?


Budge is a smooth, quick way to calculate your budget every month. Choose your expense category and swipe the amount. The swipe is calibrated to your budget for fast and calculated entry. Don't worry about filling out long, tedious forms! Our clear and vibrant visuals will keep you alert and in check with your personal finances. With a swipe and a touch, you will be able to track exactly what your spend your money on!

Features Include:

- Beautiful UI, vibrant visuals, and bright charted expense reports



Technologies: Android, Java
