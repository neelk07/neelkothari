Budge

"Simplifying how you keep track of expenses"



Budge is a smooth way to keep track of your budget. Choose your category and swipe the amount. The swipe is calibrated to your budget for fast and easy entry. No more entering in titles and long, unnecessary descriptions. Just fast easy entry! Budge also provides various different ways of visualizing your spending habits. Coming very soon to the Google Play store!



Technologies: Android, Parse SDK



Accomplist (2012)

Accomplist

"Discover. Create. Accomplist."



Accomplist is a social goal/bucketlist sharing website which allows uers to navigate and vote for their favorite items, comment on unique and interesting goals, share their own goals with others, and search for unique and inspirational bucketlists. Visit the site at accomplist.herokuapp.com



Technologies: Django, Heroku
