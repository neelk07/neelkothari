Budge

"A Quick Way To Budget!"


What if you didn't have to enter in all that information just to keep track of your budget?


Budge is a smooth, quick way to calculate your budget every month. Choose your expense category and swipe the amount. The swipe is calibrated to your budget for fast and calculated entry. Don't worry about filling out long, tedious forms! Our clear and vibrant visuals will keep you alert and in check with your personal finances. With a swipe and a touch, you will be able to track exactly what your spend your money on!



Technologies: Android, Java

Daily Good



Daily Goodness, brought to you by LookFurther, is here to make gratitude a part of your everyday. Writing down the best of your day may seem simple, but it could change your life. By emphasizing the good, by choosing to remember the positives in place of negatives, and by actively acknowledging the things you have to be thankful for you can change your perspective, change your thoughts, even change your life.

CampusCal

CampusCal is an event discovery website which allows users to navigate and search for events that they might be interested in. This includes a comphrensive map view as well as search features. Users can search by category, keywords, date, or a combination of them all. Events are either scraped from local venues or crowdsourced. Upon initial facebook login, the facebook profile is analyzed to determine the preferences of the user, to suggest better events. Visit the site at campuscal.herokuapp.com



Technologies: Django, Heroku
